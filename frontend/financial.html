NHIF / SHA Coverage

The Social Health Authority (formerly NHIF) supports coverage for cervical cancer screening and outpatient services. Contact them to confirm eligibility and provider locations.

Faraja Medical Support Fund

A fund that supports up to 50 cervical cancer patients annually in Nairobi, covering treatment costs such as surgery, chemotherapy, and radiation. Open on application basis.

TogetHER Cervical Cancer Grants

TogetHER for Health offers targeted small grants to support cervical cancer prevention in low-resource communities. RFP cycles announced periodically.
